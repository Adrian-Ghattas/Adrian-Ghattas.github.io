How I Work

I have some beginner experience with design through a few projects completed during my early university classes. While this app is no longer active, it helped me gain practical insight into basic UI design and user interaction concepts.

I enjoy combining my software engineering background with design thinking to create functional and appealing interfaces.

As I continue to grow, I look forward to applying more advanced techniques like heuristic evaluation and usability testing in my workflow.
